# Solar Policy Documents

Place solar policy documents in this directory for RAG-based analysis.

## Supported Formats

- **PDF files** (.pdf): Solar policy PDFs will be automatically extracted
- **Text files** (.txt): Policy documents in plain text format

## Policy Examples to Include

1. **Federal Tax Credit Policy (ITC)**
   - Investment Tax Credit rates and eligibility
   - Documentation requirements
   - Phase-out schedules

2. **State Incentive Programs**
   - State-specific rebates and credits
   - Performance-based incentives
   - Property tax exemptions

3. **Net Metering Policies**
   - How excess generation is credited
   - Energy storage policies
   - Time-of-use rate structures

4. **Installation Requirements**
   - Building codes and safety standards
   - Interconnection procedures
   - Warranty requirements

## Usage in RAG

When you add policy documents here, they will be:
1. Automatically indexed by the RAG system
2. Available for semantic search queries
3. Used to provide context-aware analysis

The system will retrieve relevant policy sections when answering questions about 
incentive eligibility, requirements, and benefits.
